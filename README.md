## What is Chocolat?

Chocolat is an experimental Financial Intelligence research project.

Instead of relying on a single prediction model, Chocolat operates as an Edge AI Financial Operating System.

Multiple specialist AI analysts observe the market from different perspectives. Their opinions are coordinated by the Operating System, while Safety and Portfolio Intelligence continuously monitor runtime stability and capital allocation.

The objective is not simply prediction.

**Observe. Judge. Allocate. Protect. Capture Opportunity.**
